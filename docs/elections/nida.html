Nida Nasir

Running for CSS President

Hey Mac CS! My name is Nida Nasir (she/her) and I will be running for President of the Computer Science Society for the coming academic year 2021-2022.

My goal is to work more closely with the student body to create an inclusive, engaging and accommodating environment where students feel comfortable asking questions, talking to new/unfamiliar faces and learning how to navigate life as a Mac CS student. I believe I can achieve this through the invaluable experience I have gained over the past two years as a first year rep and as the Vice President of the CSS this past year. I have worked closely alongside the President and the other executive members on council to create new and exciting opportunities to make students feel welcome during these challenging times.

I ultimately hope to use my involvement with the CSS to help elevate your university experience!
